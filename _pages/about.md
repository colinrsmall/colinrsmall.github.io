About Me
======
* I am undergradaute computer science student at the University of New Hampshire. I work with Drs. [Marek Petrik](https://ceps.unh.edu/person/marek-petrik) and [Matthew Argall](https://mypages.unh.edu/argallmr/bio).
* My primary interests are in machine learning, astronomy and astrophysics, health and medicine, [venture capital investing](https://www.rinesfund.com/), and ice hockey.
